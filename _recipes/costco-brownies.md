ingredients:
- 1 pouch Ghirardelli premium choc chip brownie mix
- 60ml water
- 39g canola / vegetable oil
- 40g butter, _melted_
- 1 medium egg

directions:
- Preheat oven to 160Â°C (top-bottom mode / not fan-forced)
- Line 9 inch square baking pan with baking paper
- Mix water, egg, oil, and butter in a medium mixing bowl until fully mixed.
- Add brownie mix. Stir until no dry patches.
- Pour into baking pan and smooth with a spatula to spread evenly.
- Bake for 40 minutes.
- Cool in pan completely before cutting.
